Bartholomeo

A: Hidden Barrier
1 devil fruit, 3 cooldown
Bartholomeo sets up an invisible barrier, for 1 turn Bartholomeo
will reflect all physical skils used on him back to their owner and 
he will ignore all nonphysical skills used on him. This skill is 
invisible.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil Fruit

B: Barrier Crash
1 devil fruit, 1 cooldown
Bartholomeo sends a barrier forward to strike an opponent, this 
skill deals 20 damage and gives Bartholomeo 10 damage reduction
for 2 turn. If "Hidden Barrier" or "Barrier" was used last turn
this skills cooldown will be decreased by 1. This damage reduction stacks.
Devil fruit/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Taunt
2 random, 5 cooldown
Batholomeo's behaviour acts like a constant taunt towards everyone
around him, for 3 turns all enemy skills used on Bartholomeo's 
allies will be reflected to Bartholomeo. During this time, enemies
strategic skills will be stunned.
Strategic/Instant

D: Barrier
1 devil fruit 1 random, 4 cooldown
Bartholomeo's devil fruit allows him to generate unbreakable 
barriers, Bartholomeo will ignore all harmful skills used on him
for 2 turns. If "Taunt" is active then all allies will gain 10 DD.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
