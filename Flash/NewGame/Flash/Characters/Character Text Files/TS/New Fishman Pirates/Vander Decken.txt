Vander Decken

A: Mark
no cost, no cooldown
Vander Decken marks one enemy making them the target of
all his skill until Vander Decken or that enemy dies, if
Mark is used on another enemy the skill will end on the
first one.
Physical/Melee/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Axe Throw
1 weapon, no cooldown
Vander Decken throws an axe at the marked enemy dealing 
25 damage the next turn and applying an affliction 
increasing non-affliction damage dealt to that enemy by 5
for 3 turns. 
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Affliction*Devil fruit

C: Mark Power
1 devil fruit, 1 cooldown
For 1 turn, Vander Decken will reflect all ranged enemy 
skills used on him to the marked enemy, this skill is
invisible to the enemy. If no skills are reflected that
enemy will instead be dealt 10 ranged weapon damage.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Noah Throw
2 devil fruit 2 random, 5 cooldown
Vander Decken throws the giant ancient ship Noah at the enemy 
team. When 3 turns have passed, all enemies will be dealt
100 damage and be stunned for 3 turns.
This skill ignores invulnerability.
This skill will end if Vander Decken's devil fruit abilities are
stunned or he dies.
Weapon/Ranged/Control/Unique*Devil fruit
