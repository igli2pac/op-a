Caesar Clown

A: Gastanet
1 weapon 1 devil fruit, no cooldown.
Caeser always carries some castanets with him, allowing him
to light the gases he can produce. Ceaser deals 40 damage
to an enemy. This move cannot be used on an enemy affected
by Karakuni.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Blue Sword
1 devil fruit 1 random, 3 cooldown
Caeser creates a lightsaber out of superheated gas and
attacks an opponent, that enemy will be dealt 15 damage
each turn for 3 turns. During this time, if that enemy 
uses a non-strategic move, they will be dealt 10 damage.
Devil Fruit/Melee/Action/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Karakuni
1 devil fruit, 5 cooldown
Caeser removes the air around him. For 4 turns, whenever an enemy uses a 
melee move on Caeser, they will be dealt 15 affliction damage and be 
stunned for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Affliction*Devil fruit

D: Gas Form
1 random, 4 cooldown
Caeser Clown becomes invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
