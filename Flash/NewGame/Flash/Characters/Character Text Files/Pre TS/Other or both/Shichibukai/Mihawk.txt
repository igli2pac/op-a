Dracule Mihawk

A: Kokuto Issen
1 weapon 1 random, no cooldown
Mihawk makes a vertical slash, dealing 30 damage to one enemy.
If that enemy had full health this skill will also stun that enemy.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

B: Kokuto Kogetsu
1 weapon 1 haki, 1 cooldown
Mihawk launches a horisontal wave at the enemy team, all enemies 
will be dealt 25 damage and any enemy that drops below 60 health
by this effect will have their nonstrategic skills stunned for
1 turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Kogatana
1 haki, 3 cooldown
Mihawk draws his smallest blade for use against weak foes, Mihawk
will ignore all skills used on him by enemies with less than 30
health. If an enemy with less than 60 health uses a skill on 
Mihawk he will deal 10 damage* to them.
This skill lasts 3 turns or until Mihawk becomes stunned.
Strategic/Control
*Weapon/Melee/Instant

D: Hawk eyes
1 random, 4 cooldown
For 1 turn, Mihawk will ignore all enemy skills used on him,
if a stun was ignored this way, Mihawk will gain 1 weapon energy.
This skill is invisible.
Strategic/Instant
