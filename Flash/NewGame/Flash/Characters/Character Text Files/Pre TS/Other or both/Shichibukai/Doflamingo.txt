Donquixote Doflamingo

A: Parasite
2 devil fruit, 5 cooldown
Doflamingo controls an opponent like a marionet. For 3 turns, 
one enemy will be stunned and Doflamingo will become invulnerable.
During this time, Doflamingo's skills will be replaced by that 
enemies skills (even unique skills). This effect cannot be ignored
by Doflamingo.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: String Beheading
1 devil fruit, no cooldown
Doflamingo uses a string to cut off an enemies head. One enemy
that has below 30 health will be dropped to 5 health. This skill cannot
be countered or reflected.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Invisible Cutting Knot
1 devil fruit, no cooldown
Doflamingo cuts an enemy with his strings while moving quickly
trough the battlefield, one enemy will recieve 10 damage and
Doflamingo will ignore all nondamage effects for 2 turns, this
effect refreshes if used while active.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Leg Block
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Doflamingo invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
