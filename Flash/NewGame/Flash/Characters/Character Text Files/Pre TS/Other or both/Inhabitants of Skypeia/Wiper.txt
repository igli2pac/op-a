Wiper

A: Burn Bazooka
1 weapon 1 random, 1 cooldown
Wiper shoots a giant blue flame at the enemy team with his bazooka, all
enemies will be dealt 20 damage and for 3 turns if an enemy has a new
affliction applied to them they won't be able to reduce damage for 2 
turns. Both of these effects refresh.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

B: Reject Dial
1 weapon 1 physical, 2 cooldown
Closing in on an enemy, Wiper releases the deadly Reject Dial destroying
both him and his enemy, one enemy will be dealt 60 piercing damage and 
Wiper will be dealt 30 affliction damage. This skill ignores 
invulnerability.
Weapon/Melee/Instant*Affliction

C: Kairoseki Skates
1 weapon, no cooldown
Wiper kicks an enemy with his skates dealing 15 damage and applying an 
affliction reducing their non-affliction damage 5 for 3 turns. This
affliction stacks.
Weapon/Melee/Instant*Affliction

D: Airborn Assassin
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Wiper invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
