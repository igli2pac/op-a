Chessmarimo

A: Dobikkuri Marimo, Cuatro Hammer
1 weapon, 4 cooldown
Chessmarimo draws 4 mallets, Chessmarimo will deal 10 damage to one enemy
each turn for 4 turns. This duration is lowered by 1 each time Chessmarimo
recieves 10 or more damage in 1 turn.
Weapon/Melee/Action

B1: Dobikkuri Marimo, Cuatro Axe
1 random, 4 cooldown
Chessmarimo wields 4 axes, this skill will be changed to "Happou Yuki 
Daore" for 3 turns and during this time, Chessmarimo will deal 5 piercing
weapon damage to any opponent that beforms a new nonstrategic move on him.
Strategic/Instant

B2: Happou Yukidaore
1 weapon, no cooldown
A special attack Chessmarimo beforms with his axes. Chessmarimo will
deal 25 piercing damage to an opponent. For 1 turn, Chessmarimo will
ignore all nondamage effects as well as all affliction damage.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

C: Yukiwarisou
1 weapon 1 random, 2 cooldown
Chessmarimo shoots a cutting wind at at the enemy team, all enemies will
recieve 15 damage and if any of them was deal damage by Chessmarimo this
turn they will be stunned for 1 turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

D: Yuki no Ya Marimo
1 random, 1 cooldown
Chessmarimo lights some arrows on fire and shoots them at an enemy, that
enemy will receive 10 damage and then, if they had any destructable 
defense, they will have an affliction applied to them dealing 5 
affliction damage each turn for 3 turns.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant
