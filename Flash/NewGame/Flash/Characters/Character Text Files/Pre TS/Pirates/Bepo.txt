Bepo

A: Kung-Fu Kick
1 physical, no cooldown
Bepo kicks an opponent, dealing 10 damage to that enemy and for every 10
damage Bepo dealt to them this turn, he will gain 5 damage reduction for
2 turns that stacks.
Physical/Melee/Instant 

B: Beary High Kick Storm
1 physical 1 random, 3 cooldown
Bepo kicks all of his opponents, each turn for 2 turn, each opponent will
be dealt 10 damage and during this time, if an ally uses a new physical
skill on an enemy, Bepo will also use "Kung-Fu Kick" on that enemy.
This skill stacks. 
Physical/Melee/Action

C: Kung-Fu Stance
2 random, no cooldown
Bepo assumes a combat stance. For 2 turns, whenever "Kung-Fu Kick" is 
used on an enemy "Beary High Kick Storm" well also be activated and if
"Beary High Kick Storm" is used "Kung-Fu Kick" will also be activated on
a random enemy. This skill refreshes.
Strategic/Instant

D: Self Guilt
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Bepo invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
