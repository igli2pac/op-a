Urouge

A: Karma
1 devil fruit, 6 cooldown
Urouge uses his devil fruit to grow in size everytime he takes
damage, for 5 turns, whenever Urouge is dealt damage, the damage
of Inga Zarashi and Pillar of Retribution will be increased by
that amount for as long as this skill is active. During this time, 
Urouge cannot receive more than 40 damage in total each turn 
(Including affliction damage).
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Inga Zarashi
1 physical, no cooldown
Urouge delivers karmatic justice, this skill will deal 5 damage
to one enemy each turn for 2 turns.
Physical/Melee/Action

C: Pillar of Retribution
1 weapon 1 random, 1 cooldown
Urouge smashes his enemies with his giant pillar. All enemies will
be dealt 5 damage and Urouge will ignore stuns for 1 turn.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

D: Massive Size 
1 random, 3 cooldown
Urouge's health cannot go below 5 for 1 turn. Damage against him
will still count towards "Karma".
This skill is invisible.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
