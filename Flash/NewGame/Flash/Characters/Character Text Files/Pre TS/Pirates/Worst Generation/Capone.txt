Capone "Gang" Bege

A: Eye-Snipers
1 devil fruit 1 random, no cooldown
Capone orders his men to snipe an enemy from his eye, one enemy
will be dealt 25 damage and this skill will have an additional
effect based on how much total destructable defense Capone has.
10+: This skill will ignore invulnerability.
25+: This skill will deal an additional 5 damage.
40+: This skill will also stun its target for 1 turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Fullbody Bombardment
1 devil fruit 1 random, no cooldown
Capones men fires a lineup of cannons at the enemy team. Capone
will deal 10 damage to each opponent and 5 extra damage for each
10 of total destructable defense he has.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Thrumpet Blast
2 random, 4 cooldown
Capone prepares his men for action, for 3 turns, "Eye-Snipers"
and "Fullbody Bombardment" will cost random energy instead of
any specific energy. Also Capone will gain 5 more destructable
defense from all skills during this time.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Living Fortress
1 Random, 2 cooldown
For 4 turns, Capone will gain 5 destructable defense each turn,
if this skill is used again while active he will gain 10 DD.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
