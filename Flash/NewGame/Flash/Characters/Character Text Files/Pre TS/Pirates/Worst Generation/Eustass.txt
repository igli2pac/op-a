Eustass Kid

A: Attract
2 random, 4 cooldown
Eustass attracts all nearby metal objects, gaining 5 DD each turn
for each enemy as well as reducing the weapon damage of enemies by
5 for the duration. This skill lasts 4 turns.
Devil fruit/Action/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Repel
1 devil fruit, 3 cooldown
Eustass will reflect the next ranged move used on him back at it's
controller, this skill lasts for 2 turns or until triggered.
This skill is invisible.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Scrap Gun
1 devil fruit, no cooldown
Eustass fires a small projectile at an enemy dealing 15 damage
to them. If Eustass has any DD, this skill will deal an additional
15 damage.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Giant Repel
1 devil fruit 1 random, no cooldown
Eustass fires off all of his gathered metal, Eustass will lose
all destructable defense and deal damage to each enemy equal
to the amount lost this way.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
