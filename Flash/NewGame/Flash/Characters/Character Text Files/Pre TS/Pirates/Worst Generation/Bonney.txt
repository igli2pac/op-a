Jewelry Bonney

A: Age Regression
1 devil fruit 1 random, 3 cooldown
Bonney turns one enemy into a small child, that enemy will receive
10 extra damage from nonaffliction skills and will have their
skills replaced with "Wail" for 2 turns. This skill cannot
be ignored. This skill cannot be used on an enemy affected by 
"Age Progression".
Physical/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

A2: Wail
1 random, no cooldown
This character tries to wail on an enemy dealing 15 damage to a
random enemy.
Physical/Melee/Instant

B: Age Progression
1 devil fruit 1 random, 3 cooldown
Bonney turns one enemy into an old geezer, one enemy will have
their nonaffliction damage decreased by 10 as well as taking
10 affliction damage each turn for 3 turns. If that enemy uses
a nonstrategic skill during this time they will be dealt 10
affliction damage. This skill cannot be used on an enemy affected
by "Age Regression". This skill cannot be ignored.
Physical/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Neverending Hunger
2 random, 2 cooldown
Bonney starts chewing down on endless amounts of food regaining 5 
health each turn. Any turn Bonney isn't dealt damage she regains
5 extra health. This skill is permanent.
This skill can be used while active to regain 30 health.
Strategic/Instant

D: Child Tackle
1 random, 5 cooldown
Bonney reverts to a more childlike state and tackles an ally out
of danger. This skill makes both Bonney and an ally invulnerable
for 1 turn. If no allies are alive this skill will cap all total 
damage dealt to Bonney to 40 for 1 turn instead.
Strategic/Instant/Unique
