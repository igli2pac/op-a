Absalom

A: Shisha No Te
1 weapon, no cooldown
Absalom uses the two invisible bazookas from under his arms to shoot 
an enemy. Absalom targets one enemy, if that enemy uses a new skill
next turn they will be dealt 30 damage. If they don't this skill will 
deal 15 damage instead. This skill is invisible until it ends.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

B: Kaijin No Te
1 physical, 4 cooldown
Absaloms launches an invisible assault on one enemy. For 2 turns, one 
enemy will have their first nonstrategic skill countered and if they 
are countered this way, Absalom will deal 20 damage to them each turn
for 2 turn*. The target of this skill is invisible.
Physical/Melee/Instant*Action

C: Skating
1 devil fruit 2 random, 5 cooldown
Absalom turns invisible, for 4 turns no enemy can target Absalom except
with skills that target all enemies. Absaloms skills will ignore 
invulnerability during this time.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Mixed Body
1 random, 4 cooldown
Absaloms strange body makes him much tougher than a regular human, 
for 1 turn, Absalom will gain 50 destructable defense and will gain 1 
random energy if he is dealt damage. This skill is invisible to the enemy.
Strategic/Instant
