Hatchan

A: Takoashi Kiken
1 weapon 1 random, 3 cooldown
Hatchan charges at an enemy while swinging his 6 swords, for the
next 3 turns Hachan will deal 15 damage to that enemy each turn.
If that enemy is affected by "Shikai Zero: Takohaci Black", 
this skill will deal 10 extra damage.
Weapon/Melee/Action

B: Takotsubo no Kamae
1 weapon, 3 cooldown
Hatchan assumes a defensive position with his swords held out,
the next enemy to use a physical or melee skill on Hatchan will
be dealt 20 damage* and receive an affliction increasing 
nonaffliction damage dealt to them by 5 for 3 turns.
This skill lasts for 2 turns or until triggered.
Strategic/Control
Weapon/Melee/Instant*Affliction

2 random, 4 cooldown
Hatchan sprays his opponents with black ink, for 2 turns, all
enemy skills that require a target will be randomized between
its legal targets.
Strategic/Instant

D: Takohachi Number Nine
1 random, 4 cooldown
Hatchan becomes invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
