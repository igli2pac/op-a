Bellamy

A: Spring Snipe
1 devil fruit 1 random, no cooldown
Bellamy flings himself at an opponent with his incredible speed, Bellamy
will deal 30 damage to one opponent, if Spring Hopper is active then 
this skill will also stun that enemy for 1 turn. This skill will deal 
10 more damage for each Pressure counter Bellamy has and all Pressure
counters are removed afterwards.
Physical/Melee/Instant/unique*Devil fruit

B: Spring Hopper
2 devil fruit, 5 cooldown
Bellamy will become invulnerable for 5 turns, this invulnerability will
end for 1 turn if Bellamy uses a new skill except "Spring Pressure".
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Spring Pressure
1 random, no cooldown
Bellamy increases the pressure of his spring legs, Bellamy will gain 1
Pressure counter. This skill will give additional characteristics to 
Spring Snipe equal to the amount of Pressure Counters on Bellamy.
2+: Piercing damage.
3+: Ignores invulnerability.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Spring Evasion
1 random, 4 cooldown
Bellamy jumps into the air, this skill makes Bellamy invulnerable for 1 
turn. If Spring Hopper is active this skill will also give give Bellamy
10 destrucable defense.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*DEvil fruit
