Captain Kuro

A: Shakushi
1 haki 1 weapon, 5 cooldown
Captain Kuro moves at a rapid pace slashing up everyone within
striking distance. All other characters take 10 damage each 
turn and Kuro will be invulnerable for 3 turns
Weapon/Melee/Control

B: Murder Slash
1 weapon, 1 cooldown
Captain Kuro slashes an enemy, dealing 20 damage to that
enemy and stunning them for 1 turn if their health becomes lower than 
50. 
Weapon/Melee/Instant

C: Murderous Intent
1 haki, 3 cooldown
Captain Kuro gains a buff, making all his moves deal 20 more 
damage for each dead ally for 3 turns.
Strategic/Instant

D: Cool Appearance
1 random, 4 cooldown
Captain Kuro becomes invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
