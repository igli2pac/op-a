Jango

A: One, Two, Jango
2 Haki, 3 cooldown
Jango uses hypnosis to make everyone in range fall asleep,
including himself. The next turn, any character that didn't
use a move during this time will be stunned for 2 turns.
(To clarify, you get 2 turns to do moves while your opponent
gets 1 (if stacked correctly))
Haki/Ranged/Control

B: Chakram Throw
1 random, no cooldown
Jango throws one of his pendulums like a chakram, cutting one
enemy for 15 damage.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Power Hypnosis
1 haki 1 random, 3 cooldown
Jango hypnotises his allies to make them stronger, their
non-affliction skills will deal 10 more damage, for 2 turns.
Strategic/Action

D: Reckless Hypnosis
1 random, 3 cooldown
Jango and one enemy becomes invulnerable for 1 turn and that enemy
will be stunned for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
