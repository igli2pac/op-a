1 weapon 1 random, no cooldown

melee moves for 1 turn and dealing 30 damage to an enemy.

B: Kyokugi! Kamikaze Hyakkoma Gekijo
1 weapon 1 random, 2 cooldown
Cabaji litters the ground with exploding tops, dealing
10 damage to every opponent each turn for 2 turns, and 
dealing 5 damage to any opponent who beforms a melee
attack.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Dirty Tactics
1 physical, no cooldown
Cabaji deals 0 damage to an enemy, that enemy will take
an additional 5 damage for every 10 health they are 
missing. Also, if that enemy took more than 20 damage from
this skill they will have an affliction applied to them
increasing damage taken from non-afflictions by 5 for 5 turns.
Physical/Melee/Instant*Affliction

D: Kyukugi! Yama Noboro
1 random, 4 cooldown

Strategic/Instant
