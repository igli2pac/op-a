Mohji

A: Whip Strike
1 random, no cooldown
Mohji whips an opponent, dealing 5 damage to them. If that 
enemy uses an offensive skill next turn, they will have an 
affliction applied to them, increasing nonaffliction damage dealt to them
by 10 for 2 turns. This skill stacks.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant*Affliction

B: Whip Snare
1 random, 2 cooldown
Mohji catches an opponent with his whip, that enemy will be
stunned for 1 turn. If that enemy is affected by Whip Strike
they will be dealt 10 damage.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Animal Training
2 random, 3 cooldown
Mohji is a selfproclaimed master of animals. Mohji tries to
tame one of his opponents, decreasing nonaffliction damage 
dealt by the target by 10 for 2 turns.
Strategic/Action

D: Riding Richie
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Mohji invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
