Alvida

A: Multi Smash
1 weapon 1 random, no cooldown
Alvida wacks two opponents over their head with her spiked club.
One target enemy and another random enemy will be dealt 25 damage
that ignores invulnerability.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

B: Overhead Smash
1 weapon 1 random, 1 cooldown
Alvida smashes one enemy over the head with her club, one enemy
will be dealt 30 damage and be stunned for 1 turn.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

C: Anger
1 random, 4 cooldown
For 3 turns, whenever Alvida is dealt damage, the damage of 
"Overhead Smash" and "Multi Smash" will be increased by 10 for 2
turns.
This skill will be activated whenever one of Alvida's allies dies and
does not stack.
Strategic/Instant

D: Intimidation
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Alvida invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
