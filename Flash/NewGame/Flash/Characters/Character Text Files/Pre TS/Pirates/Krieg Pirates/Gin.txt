Gin the "Man-Demon"

A: Shield Breaker
1 physical, no cooldown
Gin hits an enemy with one of his tonfa, removing all destructable
defense from that enemy and then dealing 10 damage to them.
Gin then gains 1 momentum charge, increasing the damage of this
skill and "Head Pound" by 5.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

B: Momentum
1 random, no cooldown
Gin swings his tonfa gaining 1 momentum charge, "Shield Breaker" 
and "Head Pound" will deal an additional 5 damage for each
momentum charge.
Strategic/Instant

C: Headlock
1 weapon 1 random, 4 cooldown
Gin nails an enemy to the ground with his tonfa, Gin uses all his
momentum charges and stuns that enemy for 1 turn for each momentum
charge he had. During this time, "Head Pound" can be used on
that enemy.
Weapon/Melee/Control

D: Head Pound
1 weapon 1 random, no cooldown
Gin smashes an enemy affected by "Headlock" dealing 20 damage
and becoming invulnerable for 1 turn. Gin will gain 1 momentum 
charge.
Weapon/Melee/Instant
