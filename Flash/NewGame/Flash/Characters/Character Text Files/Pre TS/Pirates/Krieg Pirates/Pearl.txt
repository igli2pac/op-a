Pearl

A: Pearl Present
1 physical, no cooldown
Pearl punches an enemy with one of his shields, dealing 15 damage
and gaining destructable defense equal 5 times the amount of enemies
alive.
Physical/Melee/Instant

B: Pearl Close
1 physical 1 random, no cooldown
Pearl crushes an enemies head with his shields like a cymbal,
dealing 30 damage and gaining 20 DD.
If Pearl has over 50 DD this skill will also stun for 1 turn.
Physical/Melee/Instant

C: Fire Pearl Daitokuten
1 haki 1 random, 5 cooldown
Can only be activated if Pearl has less than full health.
Pearl lights himself on fire applying an affliction to any enemy he 
attacks dealing 5 affliction damage to them each turn for 3 turns.
Any enemy that uses a melee or physical move on Pearl during this time
will also be applied this affliction.
Pearl will also gain 10 DD every turn.
This skill lasts 3 turns.
Strategic/Instant *Affliction

D: Pearl Shields
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Pearl invulnerable for 1 turn
Strategic/Instant
