Vivi

1 weapon, no cooldown
Vivi uses her peacock slashers to deal 15 damage to one opponent each 
turn for 2 turns.

B: Peacock String Slasher
1 weapon 1 random, 3 cooldown
Vivi tries to chop off an enemies head with her string slasher, dealing
10 damage each turn for 2 turns. That enemy will lose 1 random energy
each turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Action

C: Hypnosis
1 haki 1 random, 3 cooldown
Vivi uses a special perfume to paralyze an enemy, that enemy will be 
unable to target Vivi or her allies for 2 turns. That enemy will receive
10 extra damage from weapon skills during this time.
Strategic/Action

D: Carou Dash
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Vivi invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
