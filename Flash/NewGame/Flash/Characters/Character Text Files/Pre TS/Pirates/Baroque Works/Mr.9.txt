Mr.9

A: Nekketsu Nine Konjo Bat
1 weapon, 3 cooldown
Using his two bats and fast acrobatics, Mr.9 attacks an enemy dealing
10 damage each turn for 3 turns. During this time Mr.9 gains 10 damage 
reduction and that enemy will lose a random energy if they use a new skill
on Mr.9.
Weapon/Melee/Action

B: Kattobae Shikomi Bat
1 weapon, 1 cooldown
Mr.9's bats shoots out a chain to catch an enemy. That enemy will be 
stunned for 1 turn and take 10 more damage from physical and weapon 
skills.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Break Out
2 random, 4 cooldown
Mr.9 return to battle dramaticly, for 2 turns, if Mr.9 would be dealt
lethal damage by an opponents attack he will instead go to 5 health and
deal 20 damage* to that opponent. This skill is invisible until it ends
or is triggered.
Strategic/Instant
*Weapon/Melee/Instant

D: Bat Block
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Mr.9 invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
