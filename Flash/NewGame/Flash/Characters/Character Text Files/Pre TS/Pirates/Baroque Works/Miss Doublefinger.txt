Miss Doublefinger, Paula

A: Sea Urchin Stinger
1 devil fruit 1 random, 3 cooldown
Paula puffs up her hair like sea urchin and charges at her enemies. Paula
will deal 10 damage each turn to each enemy for 3 turns. During this time,
Paula cannot use any other skills except Stinger Hedgehog.
Physical/Melee/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Stinger Finger
1 devil fruit, no cooldown
Paula strikes an enemy dealing 20 damage to her target. This skill
ignores destructable defense.
Physical/Melee/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Toge Toge Doping
1 devil fruit 1 random, 4 cooldown
Paula stings both of her shoulders and pumps her arms to an
incredible size, for 4 turns Stinger Finger will deal an
additional 10 damage and stun the targets nonstrategic skills for 1 turn.
Paula cannot use her other skills except for Stinger Finger
during this time.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil Fruit

D: Stinger Hedgehog
1 devil fruit 1 random, 4 cooldown
Paula curls up her body to a spiky ball. For 4 turns, Sea Urchin
Stinger will deal an additional 5 damage and Paula will be 
invulnerable to melee skills.
Paula cannot use other skills except Sea Urchin Stinger during
this time.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil Fruit
