Diamond Jozu

A: Cabochon Knuckle
1 random, no cooldown
Jozu punches an enemy with a diamond encrusted fist, that enemy will be 
dealt 15 damage and Jozu will gain 10 damage reduction for 1 turn.
Jozu will gain one "Dispersion Impact" counter.
Physical/Melee/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Brilliant Punk
1 devil fruit, no cooldown
Jozu charges an opponent. One enemy will be dealt 20 damage that ignores 
invulnerability. If this skill was used by "Radiant Jozu" it will deal 25 
damage instead. 
Jozu will gain one "Dispersion Impact" counter.
Physical/Melee/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Dispersion Impact
1 devil fruit 1 random, no cooldown
Jozu throws a giant block of ice at his opponents, all enemies will be dealt
10 damage and for every "Dispersion Impact" counter Jozu have he will gain
5 permanent damage reduction. Jozu will then lose all "Dispersion Impact"
counters. This damage reduction ends if Jozu is fully stunned.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Radiant Jozu
1 random, 4 cooldown
Jozu's devil fruit allows him to partially cover his body in diamonds, for
1 turn, Jozu will gain 100 damage reduction and and enemy that uses a 
nonstrategic skill on Jozu will have "Briliant Punk" used on them.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
