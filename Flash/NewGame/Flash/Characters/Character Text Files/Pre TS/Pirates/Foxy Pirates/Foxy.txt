Foxy the Silver Fox

A: Noro Noro Beam
1 devil fruit 1 random, 4 cooldown
Foxy slows down the enemy team. For 3 turns all enemies will have their 
cooldowns increased by 3 and anytime a skill is used on them, they will
ignore it for 3 turns and then be applied it again.
Strategic/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Megaton Kyubi Rush
1 physical, no cooldown
Foxy rushes in with a quick flurry of punches. One enemy affected by 
Noro Noro Beam will be dealt 30 damage.
Physical/Melee/Instant

C: Foxy Fighter
1 weapon, no cooldown
Foxy shoots a cannonball at an enemy at slow it down, one enemy will
be dealt 25 damage next turn. The target of this skill is invisible.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit

D: Defensive Beam
1 random, 4 cooldown
Foxy slows down an enemies attack, for 1 turn if an enemy uses a new skill
on Foxy they will be stunned for 1 turn, this skill bypasses Noro Noro
Beam. This skill is invisible.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
