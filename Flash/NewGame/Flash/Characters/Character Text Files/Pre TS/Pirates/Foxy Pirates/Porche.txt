Porche

A: Ohana Shuriken
1 haki, 1 cooldown
Porche shoots some disguised shurikens at her opponents, all enemies will
be dealt 10 damage each turn for 2 turns. During this time, if any enemy 
gains destructable defense or damage reduction "Ohana Shuriken" will be
refreshed on that enemy. This skill stacks up to three times.
Weapon/Ranged/Action

B: Cutie Baton
1 random, 3 cooldown
Porche swings her baton, for 3 turns "Ohana Shuriken" will deal 5 more
damage and have no cooldown. During this time, Porche will gain 10 damage
reduction.
Strategic/Instant

C: Ohana Saimin
1 haki 1 random, 3 cooldown
Porche intoxicates an enemy, for 3 turns, that enemy will have their
skills that cost 2 or more energy stunned.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

D: Celebration
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Porche invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
