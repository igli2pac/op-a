Marine Soldiers

A: Ute!
2 random, no cooldown
Marine Soldiers fire their rifles dealing 30 damage to target 
enemy. This skill will deal 5 extra damage if Marine Soldiers have
full health.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

B: Close Quarters Combat
1 random, 3 cooldown
Marine soldiers run in to attack dealing 10 damage to each 
opponent each turn for 3 turns. This skills ends if the Marine 
Soldiers takes 30 or more damage in one turn.
Weapon/Melee/Action

C: Ambush
1 random, 4 cooldown
Marine Soldiers target one enemy, if that enemy uses a non-strategic
skill next turn they will be stunned for 2 turns and take 10 extra
damage from Marine Soldiers skills. This skill is invisible.
Strategic/Instant

D: Meat Shields
1 random, 4 cooldown
All skills used on target ally is redirected to Marine Soldiers 
for one turn.
Strategic/Instant
