Fukuro

A: Soru "Tekkai Dama"
1 Physical, 1 cooldown
Combining The Tekkai and Soru techniques Fukuro flies towards an enemy 
dealing 15 damage ignoring afflictions for 2 turns. 
Cannot be used during Jugon Ogi: "Fukuro Dataki".
Physical/Melee/Instant

B: Jugon
1 Physical 1 random, 1 cooldown
Fukuro uses the Shigan technique with a full fist, one enemy will be 
dealt 30 damage and if Fukuro deals damage to that enemy during the next
3 turns, they will be stunned for 1 turn. This skill renews.
Physical/Melee/Instant

C: Jugon Ogi: "Fukuro Dataki"
1 Physical 1 random, 4 cooldown
Combining Soru and Shigan, Fukuro attacks one enemy from 
all angles dealing 20 damage to them each turn and 
gaining 15 damage reduction for 3 turns. During this time
Fukuro cannot use Soru "Tekkai Dama". Can be interrupted
by stuns.
Physical/Melee/Action

D: Kami-e "Slime"
1 Random, 4 cooldown
Fukuri uses a special version of Kami-E to become invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
