Kaku

A: Rankyaku "Ran"
1 Random, 3 cooldown
Kaku slices the air to launch many small waves at the
enemy dealing 5 piercing damage each turn to every enemy 
for 3 turns. An enemy that takes damage from this move
is unable to become invulnerable for 1 turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Action

B: Rankyaku "Hakurai"
2 Random, 1 cooldown
Haku creates a larger Rankyaku blade which deals 30 damage to one 
character, if Rankyaku "Ran" is active on that enemy, this skill will have
no cooldown.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

Kaku transforms into his half giraffe form, switching his
moveset, this skill will become Human Form after use.
Devil fruit/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
