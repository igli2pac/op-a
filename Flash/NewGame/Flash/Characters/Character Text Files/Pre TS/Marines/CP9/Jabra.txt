Jabra

A: Jusshigan
1 Devil fruit, no cooldown
Using his ten claws to beform a Shigan on one enemy Jabra
deals 20 damage and increases the cost of that enemies skills by 
one random for 2 turns, this effect stacks.
Physical/Melee/Instant

B: Rankyaku "Lupus Fall"
1 Weapon 1 Devil Fruit, 2 cooldown
Jabra fires 4 wolf-shaped Rankyaku blades against an enemy
dealing 40 damage to that enemy and increasing the cost 
of their skills by 2 random for 2 turns.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Wolf's Lie
1 willpower, 3 cooldown
Jabra tells a lie to a opponent to make them let their
guard down, that enemy will deal 10 less damage for 2
turns and have the cost of their skills increased by 1 
Willpower for the duration.
Strategic/Instant

D: Tekkai
1 random, 4 cooldown
Jabra becomes invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
