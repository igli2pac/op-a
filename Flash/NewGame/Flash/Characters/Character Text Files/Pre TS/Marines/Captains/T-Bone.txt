2 weapon, 3 cooldown
T-Bone unleases a thundering wave of vengeance from his sword
dealing damage equal to 5 times the amount of 10's his allies are 
missing to one enemy.

deal (5 + 3) * 5 = 40 damage, and if both allies are dead it will 
deal 100 damage)
Weapon/Ranged/Instant/Unique
