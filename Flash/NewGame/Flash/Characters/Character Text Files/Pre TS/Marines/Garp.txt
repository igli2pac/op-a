Monkey D. Garp

A: Ai aru Kobushi
1 haki, no cooldown
Garp punches an enemy with a haki inbued punch dealing 20 damage.
This skill ignores all bonuses gained from devil fruit abilities, except
destructable defense.
Physical/Melee/Instant*Haki

B: Genkotsu Giant Iron Meteor
1 weapon 1 haki, 4 cooldown
Garp throws a giant iron ball at the enemy team. The next turn all 
enemies will be dealt 30 damage, the cost of their skills will be 
increased by 1 random for 2 turns.
Any characters that had destructable defense will be dealt an
additional 15 damage and be stunned for 1 turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Genkotsu Ryuseigun
1 weapon 1 random, 1 cooldown
Garp throws 1000 cannonballs at the enemy team dealing 15 damage
to each opponent each turn for 2 turns. Enemies dealt damage by
this skill will be unable to become invulnerable for 1 turn.
Weapon/Ranged/Action

D: Old Man's Rest
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Garp invulnerable for 1 turn.
If Garp used "Ai aru Kobushi" last turn this skill will also heal
Garp 10 health.
Strategic/Instant
