Tashigi

A: Cut of Justice
1 random, no cooldown
Tashigi attacks one enemy dealing 15 damage and if that enemy is stunned
she will gain 1 random energy.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

B: Arrest
1 haki 1 random, 5 cooldown
Tashigi arrests one wounded enemy, this skill can only be used on an 
enemy that has 50 or less health. That enemy will be stunned for 3 turns.
Strategic/Instant 

C: Intercept
1 haki, 2 cooldown
Tashigi intercept an enemy, for 1 turn, Tashigi will counter any new
harmful skill they use and deal 15 damage to them. This skill is
invisible.
Weapon/Melee/Instant

D: Defensive Stance
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Tashigi invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
