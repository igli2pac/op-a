Kuzan - Aokiji

A: Ice Age
2 random, 4 cooldown
Aokiji freezes the battlefield, covering everything in ice,
for the next three turns, nonaffliction damage dealt to allies
is reduced by 5 and (na) damage dealt by enemies are reduced
by 5. If Ice Saber is used on an enemy during this time,
it will deal an additional 5 damage.
Strategic/Action/Unique*Devil fruit

B: Ice Saber
1 devil fruit, 2 cooldown
Aokiji forms a sword of ice, dealing 10 damage to one enemy
every turn for 2 turns. During this time, nonaffliction damage
dealt by that enemy is decreased by 10. 
Devil Fruit/Melee/Action/Unique*Devil fruit

C: Ice Time
1 devil fruit 1 random, 6 cooldown
Aokiji incapsules an enemy in ice, stunning them for 5 turns
or until they have 3 new moves used on them (includes both 
friendly and offensive moves).
Devil fruit/Melee/Instant/Unique*devil fruit

D: Ice Form
1 random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Aokiji invulnerable for 1 turn.
If Ice Age is active, "Ice Saber" will deal 5 more damage for 1 
turn.
Strategic/Instant/Unique*Devil fruit
