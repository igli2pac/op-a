Pacifista

A: Palm Beam
1 weapon, no cooldown.
The Pacifista fires a beam from its palm dealing 30 damage to the enemy
with the most health, this is randomized if more enemies are tied for
the lead.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

B: Mouth Beam
1 Weapon 1 Random, 1 cooldown.
Next turn, the Pacifista will deal 40 damage to a random enemy that used 
a new nonstrategic skill.
Weapon/Ranged/Instant

C: Random Fire
3 random, no cooldown.
Can only be activated when below 40 HP.
The Pacifista shoots randomly in all directions dealing 30 damage* to a 
random enemy each turn until Pacifista dies. The pacifista will be 
unaffected by all harmful effect except damage during this time.
Strategic/Action
* Weapon/Ranged/Instant

D: Iron Body
1 Random, 4 cooldown
This skill makes Pacifista invulnerable for 1 turn.
Strategic/Instant
